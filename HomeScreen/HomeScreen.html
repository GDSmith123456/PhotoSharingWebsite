Welcome to Construct
Now you can easily Upload your images, have control over their metadata and share them with your friends.
Upload
Get started by uploading your images to the site. By just dragging them onto the site or selecting them from the device. Click upload and your done. As easy as that.
View
The view option allows you to easily View your uploaded items in the database. You can then access the metadata and view it from this terminal.
Delete
Deleting your images is as easy as clicking and selecting delete. They will be permanantley removed so be careful.
Share
Its now easy to share you newly uploaded images to friends by just clicking share and sharing them to the email. This will allow them to have access to the new images metadata and view and share these images aswell.
